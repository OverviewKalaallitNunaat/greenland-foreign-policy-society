Vores Formål

Nunanut is an independent platform for foreign policy analysis, dialogue, and civil society engagement in Greenland. We work to promote informed public debate and strengthen local perspectives in international relations.

Nunanut tassaavoq Kalaallit Nunaanni nunanut allanut tunngasunik misissueqqissaarnermut, oqaloqatigiinnermut inuiaqatigiillu akuliunnerannut attuumassuteqanngitsumik nittartagaq. Inuiaqatigiit oqallinnerat paasissutissanillu tunngaveqarnerunissaat sulissutigaarput.

Nunanut er en uafhængig platform for udenrigspolitisk analyse, dialog og civilsamfundsengagement i Grønland. Vi arbejder for at fremme en oplyst offentlig debat og styrke lokale perspektiver i internationale relationer.

The Board Siulersuisut Bestyrelsen

Malik Koch Hansen

Chairman Siulittaasoq Formand

Malik holds a MA in Arctic Social Sciences, during which he spent half a year at the Greenland representation in Reykjavík gaining insight into the intricate foreign diplomatic mission of Greenland. His interest in foreign policy also led him to study at the Graduate School of International Relations at the International University of Japan.

Malik Peter Koch Hansen Issittumi Inuiaqatigiilerinermi MA-qarpoq, ilinniarnerminilu ukiup affaa Reykjavíkimi Kalaallit Nunaata aallartitaqarfianiilluni nunatta diplomatiikkut sulineranik paasisimasaqarnerulerpoq. Nunanut allanut politikkimik soqutiginninnerata Japanimi International University-mut ingerlatippaa.

Malik Peter Koch Hansen har en MA i Arktisk Samfundsvidenskab, hvorunder han tilbragte et halvt år ved Grønlands repræsentation i Reykjavík for at få indsigt i Grønlands diplomatiske arbejde. Hans interesse for udenrigspolitik førte ham til Graduate School of International Relations ved International University of Japan.

Simon Bredsgaard Byrith

Vice Chairman Siulittaasup tullia Næstformand

Simon Bredsgaard Byrith holds a degree from the University of Southern Denmark specializing in international security, public law, and sanctions policy. Having moved to Nuuk in June 2024, he brings experience from Venstre’s secretariat at the Folketinget and from Brussels working with agriculture and food policy. He is currently employed by the Government of Greenland, focusing on international affairs and Arctic administration.

Simon Bredsgaard Byrith Syddansk Universitetimi ilinniagaqarpoq isumannaallisaaneq, inatsisilerneq sanktioninullu politikki sammivigalugit. 2024-mi juunimi Nuummut nuuppoq, Venstrep Folketingimi allattoqarfianit Bruxellesimilu nunalerinermut nerisassaqarnermullu politikki pillugu misilittagaqarluni. Maanna Namminersorlutik Oqartussani atorfeqarpoq nunat tamalaat pissutaat Issittumilu aqutsineq suliaqarfigalugit.

Simon Bredsgaard Byrith er uddannet fra Syddansk Universitet med speciale i international sikkerhed, folkeret og sanktionspolitik. Simon flyttede til Nuuk i juni 2024 og har erfaring fra Venstres folketingssekretariat samt fra Bruxelles med landbrug og fødevarer. Han er nu ansat i Grønlands Selvstyre, hvor han beskæftiger sig med internationale forhold og arktisk administration.

Emilie Krusell

Treasurer Aningaaserisoq Kasserer

Emilie Krusell holds a degree in sociology specializing in organizations and politics from the University of Copenhagen. She has previously been Assistant Attaché at Denmark's permanent mission to the UN in Geneva, student assistant in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and project officer in the International Department of the Red Cross.

Emilie Krusell Københavnip Universitetianiit sociologiimi ilinniagaqarpoq. Siusinnerusukkut FN-ip Genevemi aallartitaqarfiani Assistant Attachétut atorfeqarnikuuvoq, kiisalu Nunanut Allanut Naalakkersuisoqarfimmi aamma Aappalaartumi (Røde Kors) sulisarsimalluni.

Emilie Krusell er uddannet sociolog med speciale i organisation og politik fra Københavns Universitet. Hun har tidligere været Assistant Attaché ved Danmarks faste mission ved FN i Genève, studentermedhjælper i Udenrigsministeriet og projektmedarbejder i Røde Kors' internationale afdeling.

Ann Storm Rubæk

Board Member Siulersuisuni ilaasortaq Bestyrelsesmedlem

Ann Storm Rubæk holds a degree from the University of Southern Denmark in Odense and from the University of Nottingham in China, specializing in International relations and Diplomacy. She has previously been employed as a civil servant in the Premier's Office and has lived in Australia, China and now Greenland.

Ann Storm Rubæk Nunat Tamalaat akornanni pissutsinut diplomatiimullu ilinniagaqarpoq. Siusinnerusukkut Naalakkersuisut Siulittaasuata Naalakkersuisoqarfiani atorfeqarnikuuvoq, Australienimi, Kinami maannalu Kalaallit Nunaanni najugaqarnikuulluni.

Ann Storm Rubæk er uddannet fra SDU og University of Nottingham i Kina med speciale i internationale relationer og diplomati. Hun har tidligere været ansat som embedsmand i Formandens Departement og har boet i Australien, Kina og nu Grønland.

Mikkel Østergaard

Board Member Siulersuisuni ilaasortaq Bestyrelsesmedlem

Mikkel Underlin Østergaard holds a degree from Aalborg University specializing in Arctic studies and international relations. He has previously researched 'free association' in a Greenlandic context and the opportunities and challenges that Greenland would face in this.

Mikkel Underlin Østergaard Aalborg Universitetimi Issittumi pissutsinut nunallu tamalaat akornanni pissutsinut immikkut ilinniagaqarpoq. 'Free association' Kalaallit Nunaannut tunngatillugu periarfissai unammilligassaalu misissoqqissaarnikuuai.

Mikkel Underlin Østergaard er uddannet fra Aalborg Universitet med speciale i arktiske studier og internationale relationer. Han har forsket i 'free association' i en grønlandsk kontekst og de muligheder og udfordringer, Grønland ville stå over for i den forbindelse.

Dorte Munch

Board Member Siulersuisuni ilaasortaq Bestyrelsesmedlem

Dorte Munch has worked at the Government of Greenland in various positions this past interesting decade. Recognizing the need for international participation and understanding from inside Greenland she took a seat at the board.

Dorte Munch ukiuni qulini kingullerni Namminersorlutik Oqartussani assigiinngitsunik atorfeqarnikuuvoq. Nunat tamalaat akornanni peqataanerup Kalaallit Nunaata iluaniit paasineqarnerunissaa pisariaqartoq takullugu siulersuisuni ilaasortanngorpoq.

Dorte Munch har arbejdet i Grønlands Selvstyre i forskellige stillinger gennem det sidste årti. Da hun ser et behov for international deltagelse og forståelse indefra Grønland, valgte hun at træde ind i bestyrelsen.
