Diplomatic Horizons: Greenland’s New Voice

By Malik Peter Koch Hansen • 20 May 2026

As international interest in the Arctic reaches unprecedented levels, Greenland is moving beyond passive geography toward active diplomacy.

The establishment of the Greenland Foreign Policy Society marks a pivotal moment in our civil society's history. We are no longer just a subject of discussion in foreign capitals; we are the authors of our own narrative.

Our goal is to ensure that the strategic value of our land is matched by the strategic depth of our dialogue. By fostering an independent platform for analysis, we provide the tools for a more informed and sovereign future.

Author

Malik Peter Koch Hansen

Malik Peter Koch Hansen is the Chair of the Greenland Foreign Policy Society (Nunanut Allanut Politikkeqarnermut Peqatigiiffik). He works to bridge the gap between civil society and international diplomatic relations.
